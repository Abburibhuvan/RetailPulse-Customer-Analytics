# RetailPulse Project - EDA Insights

## Project Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis was performed on the online retail transactional dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, revenue patterns, product performance, geographic contribution, and business opportunities.

---

## 1. Business Type Understanding
The dataset indicates a volume-driven retail business primarily dealing with affordable products.

Observations:
- Most transactions involve small purchase quantities.
- Most products are low-priced.
- Most transaction values are relatively low.

Business Meaning:
The business depends more on high sales volume than premium product pricing.

---

## 2. Revenue Drivers
Quantity shows a strong positive relationship with revenue.

Evidence:
- Correlation between Quantity and TotalPrice = 0.83
- Scatter plot confirms higher quantities generally increase revenue.

Business Meaning:
Bulk purchasing significantly drives business revenue.

Recommendation:
Promote bundle offers, quantity discounts, and bulk purchase incentives.

---

## 3. Product Pricing Insights
Unit price shows only a weak relationship with revenue.

Evidence:
- Correlation between UnitPrice and TotalPrice = 0.14

Business Meaning:
Expensive products alone do not strongly drive total revenue.
Low-cost products can generate high revenue through bulk sales.

Recommendation:
Maintain focus on fast-moving affordable products along with premium items.

---

## 4. Bulk Buyer Behavior
Customers purchasing larger quantities mostly buy cheaper products.

Evidence:
- Quantity vs UnitPrice scatter analysis

Business Meaning:
Bulk buyers prefer low-cost products.

Recommendation:
Design wholesale offers and volume discounts around inexpensive products.

---

## 5. Product Performance Insights
Top products by quantity and top products by revenue are not identical.

Top volume products:
- WORLD WAR 2 GLIDERS ASSTD DESIGNS
- WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER

Top revenue products:
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER

Business Meaning:
Different products serve different business roles:
- volume drivers
- revenue drivers
- star products

Recommendation:
Prioritize inventory and promotions accordingly.

---

## 6. Geographic Insights
The United Kingdom contributes the highest revenue by a large margin.

Business Meaning:
The business is heavily dependent on the UK market.

Risk:
High geographic concentration risk.

Recommendation:
Expand focus into other international markets such as Germany, France, Netherlands, and Australia.

---

## 7. Seasonal Revenue Trends
Revenue increases significantly from September to November.

Peak Month:
- November

Business Meaning:
Strong seasonal demand likely due to holiday/festive shopping.

Recommendation:
Prepare inventory, staffing, logistics, and promotions before peak season.

---

## 8. Weekly Sales Pattern
Revenue is highest during midweek.

Top Day:
- Thursday

Strong Days:
- Tuesday
- Wednesday
- Thursday

Weakest Day:
- Saturday

Business Meaning:
Customer purchasing activity is concentrated midweek.

Recommendation:
Plan campaigns and operational support around high-performing weekdays.

---

## 9. Hourly Purchasing Behavior
Revenue peaks during daytime business hours.

Peak Hour:
- 12 PM

Strong Window:
- 10 AM to 3 PM

Business Meaning:
Customers shop mostly during daytime.

Recommendation:
Schedule advertisements, promotions, and support during peak hours.

---

## 10. Customer Value Insights
A small number of customers contribute disproportionately to total revenue.

Highest Value Customer:
- Customer ID 18102

Business Meaning:
VIP customer dependency exists.

Risk:
Revenue loss if top customers are lost.

Recommendation:
Implement retention strategies, loyalty rewards, and personalized engagement.

---

## 11. Customer Segmentation Opportunity
Customer spending behavior varies.

Observed Segments:
- high spend / low frequency customers
- repeat moderate spend customers
- VIP customers

Business Meaning:
Customer segmentation is possible.

Recommendation:
Use customer segmentation techniques such as RFM analysis in the next project phase.

---

## Final Business Summary
The business is a volume-driven international retail business focused on affordable products.
Revenue depends strongly on purchase quantity rather than product price.
Customer activity is seasonal, concentrated during late-year months, midweek, and daytime business hours.
Revenue is highly dependent on the UK market and a small number of high-value customers.

Key opportunities:
- bulk sales optimization
- customer retention
- international market expansion
- seasonal planning
- product strategy optimization
